Banking Management System Dataflow Diagram

June 28 2017
  • Posted By: namita
  • Comments: 0
Posted By namita on June 28, 2017

Banking Management System Data flow diagram is often used as a preliminary step to create an overview of the Banking without going into great detail, which can later be elaborated.it normally consists of overall application dataflow and processes of the Banking process. It contains all of the userflow and their entities such all the flow of Customer, Employees, Accounts, Fixed Deposit, Saving Accounts, Current Account, Balance. All of the below diagrams has been used for the visualization of data processing and structured design of the Banking process and working flow.

Zero Level Data flow Diagram(0 Level DFD) of Banking Management System :

This is the Zero Level DFD of Banking Management System, where we have eloborated the high level process of Banking. It’s a basic overview of the whole Banking Management System or process being analyzed or modeled. It’s designed to be an at-a-glance view of Saving Accounts,Current Account and Balance showing the system as a single high-level process, with its relationship to external entities of Customer, Employees and Accounts. It should be easily understood by a wide audience, including Customer, Accounts and Saving Accounts In zero leve DFD of Banking Management System, we have described the high level flow of the Banking system.

High Level Entities and proccess flow of Banking Management System:

  • Managing all the Customer
  • Managing all the Employees
  • Managing all the Accounts
  • Managing all the Fixed Deposit
  • Managing all the Saving Accounts
  • Managing all the Current Account
  • Managing all the Balance

First Level Data flow Diagram(1st Level DFD) of Banking Management System :

First Level DFD (1st Level) of Banking Management System shows how the system is divided into sub-systems (processes), each of which deals with one or more of the data flows to or from an external agent, and which together provide all of the functionality of the Banking Management System system as a whole. It also identifies internal data stores of Balance, Current Account, Saving Accounts, Fixed Deposit, Accounts that must be present in order for the Banking system to do its job, and shows the flow of data between the various parts of Customer, Accounts, Current Account, Balance, Saving Accounts of the system. DFD Level 1 provides a more detailed breakout of pieces of the 1st level DFD. You will highlight the main functionalities of Banking.

Main entities and output of First Level DFD (1st Level DFD):

  • Processing Customer records and generate report of all Customer
  • Processing Employees records and generate report of all Employees
  • Processing Accounts records and generate report of all Accounts
  • Processing Fixed Deposit records and generate report of all Fixed Deposit
  • Processing Saving Accounts records and generate report of all Saving Accounts
  • Processing Current Account records and generate report of all Current Account
  • Processing Balance records and generate report of all Balance

Second Level Data flow Diagram(2nd Level DFD) of Banking Management System :

DFD Level 2 then goes one step deeper into parts of Level 1 of Banking. It may require more functionalities of Banking to reach the necessary level of detail about the Banking functioning. First Level DFD (1st Level) of Banking Management System shows how the system is divided into sub-systems (processes). The 2nd Level DFD contains more details of Balance, Current Account, Saving Accounts, Fixed Deposit, Accounts, Employees, Customer.

Low level functionalities of Banking Management System

  • Admin logins to the system and manage all the functionalities of Banking Management System
  • Admin can add, edit, delete and view the records of Customer, Accounts, Saving Accounts, Balance
  • Admin can manage all the details of Employees, Fixed Deposit, Current Account
  • Admin can also generate reports of Customer, Employees, Accounts, Fixed Deposit, Saving Accounts, Current Account
  • Admin can search the details of Employees, Saving Accounts, Current Account
  • Admin can apply different level of filters on report of Customer, Fixed Deposit, Saving Accounts
  • Admin can tracks the detailed information of Employees, Accounts, Fixed Deposit, , Saving Accounts
